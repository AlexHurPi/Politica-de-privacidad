Policy for Seller's Quick Calculator

At Seller's Quick Calculator, we respect the privacy of our users. This policy outlines how we handle information within our application.

Data Collection

*Automatically Collected Data:

We do not collect any personal information from users. The app does not request special permissions or access data stored on the device beyond what is strictly necessary for its basic functionality.

*Temporary Data:

User-entered data (such as formulas and results) is stored locally on the device to enhance the user experience when restarting the app.

Use of Information

*The data entered into the app is used solely for performing calculations and displaying results.

*The sharing functionality allows users to voluntarily send the formula and result through WhatsApp or other services without storing or sending this data to external servers by our app.

Advertising

*The app displays banner ads provided by a third-party service. Ad providers may collect anonymous information to personalize ads according to their privacy policies.

Policy Updates

*We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be published in this section of the app.
